Welcome to our machine learning project using Teachable Machine! This project showcases our journey in teaching a model to recognize various objects and categories. Explore our process and try the model yourself.

Reflections and Lessons Learned

Inspired by Buolamwini's book “Unmasking AI”, our project aims to explore the ethical implications of AI and machine learning. Here are some key lessons we applied:

  • Algorithmic Bias: We critically audited our data to identify and reduce biases in representation.
  • Transparency: Documentation and public testing of our model were central to our design process.
  • Inclusivity: We considered diverse user perspectives to ensure a fair and ethical application.

In completing this assignment, we gained a deeper understanding of the impact of algorithm design on society, guided by the insights from Buolamwini's work.
