What is Intersectionality?

Intersectionality is a framework that explores how different social identities like race, gender, and class overlap and create unique experiences of discrimination and privilege. Learn more

What is Implicit Bias?

Implicit bias refers to unconscious attitudes or stereotypes that affect our understanding, actions, and decisions in an unconscious manner. Learn more

What is IBT?

Implicit Bias Training (IBT) focuses on identifying and mitigating unconscious biases that may impact decision-making processes in professional and personal settings. Learn more

Our Reflections on the Learning

Xiaoyu Guo: Exploring the concept of intersectionality was truly eye-opening for me. It’s a reminder that our identities are complex, and how we’re perceived in society isn’t just based on one factor but a mix of many. This learning experience encouraged me to think more critically about fairness in AI.
